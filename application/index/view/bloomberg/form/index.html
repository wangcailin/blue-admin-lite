We don't have 217 years to wait. #PressForProgress today.
The World Economic Forum estimates that it will take 217 years for economic gender parity to be achieved.

In honor of International Women's Day on March 8th, we're asking all of you to share how you will take action towards gender parity. What will you do to #PressForProgress?

On this day and throughout March, we invite you to share ideas about how you can make a difference. Make a pledge. You can choose to speak up, be bold, be more involved, teach - the opportunities to commit are endless.

Your pledges may be selected to be featured on our website and on social media.
